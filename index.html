"Inspiring minds, shaping the future"

Founded in 2001, Horizon School is located in Belgaum, Karnataka At Horizon School , our mission is to provide a high-quality education that prepares our students for success in college and beyond.

Our faculty consists of dedicated and experienced educators who are committed to helping each student reach their full potential. In addition to our core academic programs, we offer a range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, and clubs, to help our students develop their interests and talents.

Horizon School is accredited by the KEM and is a member of the KEM association. We are proud to have a diverse student body and are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment for all of our students.

Thank you for considering Horizon School for your child's education. If you have any questions or would like to schedule a tour, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to meeting you and welcoming you to our community.
